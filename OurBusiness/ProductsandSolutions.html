RFID Based Solutions for Parking & Access/ Border Control

A range of products and technologies are employed in our eParking and access control solutions with Passive RFID & ALPR technologies.  The system detects all sorts of cars and had a high accuracy.  It works indoors and outdoors and needs little maintenance.  A typical Parking management system would include the RFID readers, open entry gate automatically without time-delay, CCTV cameras, and the necessary IT infrastructure.

Intelligent Transportation System with RFID

Smart transportation will be a key aspect of future smart city management. ETC and value-added traffic information services are key smart transportation applications. On a basic ITS architecture, a diverse range of application services can be offered.

 
For traffic data collection, a typical ITS application would include the RFID readers and antennas installed on intersections which could output traffic volumes, speed and travel time.

Vietnam

As the nation is undergoing some rapid industrialization, Vietnam is currently constructing more than 2500 km of tolled highway. With the expected increase in traffic volume and the need to implement digital payment infrastructure, the government decided to rollout electronic toll collection across its existing and future highway network.

We advise the Vietnamese national ETC operator in the development of an ETC master implementation plan. We also constructed the nation’s first RFID-based ETC pilot site in Central Vietnam. The newly built pilot site forms the basis for the future national ETC system from coexist barrier-based ETC with manual toll collection to All-Electronic MLFF ETC.

Furthermore, based on the evaluation and assessment of the functional, technical, social economical and financial aspects of the tolling mechanism, we help the national operators to build up its ETC system architecture, business model, operation model, and policies and regulation strategies.  

With the ambitious plan to transform its manual toll collection lanes to ETC, Vietnam will become the shining beacon of smart city transformation for the rest of South East Asia to follow.

Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is undergoing the process of modernization. The state’s development plan for 2013-2022 called for the length of toll roads to reach 6982 km. This rapid increase in toll road length poses a major challenge on the national budget with the ever-increasing maintenance requirements.

We are in the process of providing advisory support to the development of the national ETC system in the form of a complete Feasibility Study and the development of the nation’s ETC Operational and Contractual model as well as the tender strategy for future system deployment. This entails analysis and design that allows the system to successfully transition from the current state of sectional toll roads on key corridors to a nationwide e-tolling scheme. This will require the progression from 1st generation to 2nd generation with tolling based on a hybrid barrier-based, SLFF to All-Electronic MLFF.
